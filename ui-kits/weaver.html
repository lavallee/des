Where the qualifier goes.

Eighty-three claims followed from wire to rewrite to re-rewrite. We traced which caveats survived the journey.

T. Ogunsola 14 min read Methodology · download ↓ confidence · 0.89

Across 83 news events tracked in March, every single one shed at least one qualifier between its original publication and its third re-publication. The figures below disaggregate what went missing and where.

What kind of qualifier goes missing?

The most-dropped phrase was "internal estimate, pending publication" — lost in 31 of 42 cases where a qualifier was stripped. The second most-dropped was "according to officials familiar with the matter," lost in 18 cases.
