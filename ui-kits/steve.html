The claim aged out of verifiability.

How a qualifier disappeared between Ankara, a wire desk, and the third paper that ran the number — and what it cost the record.

By K. Park · T. Ogunsola 8 min read Confidence · 0.84 Rev. 3

Three weeks ago, a deputy press officer in Ankara referenced a demographic figure during a briefing. It was picked up by two wire services and, by the time it reached the third, had dropped its qualifier. The number kept the authority of the original source while losing every caveat the briefing attached to it.

This is not a failure of a single reporter. It is a failure of the ecosystem to preserve provenance at the speed it moves. barnowl exists to keep the qualifier attached — to treat the phrase "according to internal estimates, pending verification" as part of the fact itself, not cruft to be trimmed.
