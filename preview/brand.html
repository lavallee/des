The wordmark is the logo

There is no bug, no monogram, no iconographic lockup. Every tool in the family renders its name in Instrument Serif italic — and that, at 20px in a header, is the logo. The lower-case wordmark signals engineered humility: names are tools, not monuments.

Do / don't

✓ Do
barnowl

Instrument Serif italic, sentence/lowercase, color --text. Kerning untouched. The mark earns its presence from the typeface.

✗ Don't
BARNOWL

No uppercase, no bold sans substitute, no gradient containers, no enclosing mark. The wordmark is the logo — don't build a cage around it.
