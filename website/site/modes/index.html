03
Shared floor

Expression changes. Responsibility does not.

Honest proof

No invented people, customers, metrics, testimonials, citations, or capabilities. Unknowns and fixtures are labeled.

Reachable states

Controls work. Loading, empty, error, success, and recovery states match the real flow.

Accessible structure

Semantics, focus, contrast, reduced motion, and supported widths are part of the implementation—not review polish.

Evidence custody

Claims, observations, synthesis, recommendations, actions, and outcomes remain distinct and linked.
