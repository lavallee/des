01 The premise

Taste is not one set of bans.

DES gives every surface the same ethical and usability floor. It does not give every surface the same personality.

Shared: honest proof, semantic structure, reachable states, contrast, focus, recovery, and evidence custody.

Selected: audience, primary job, task anatomy, and the starting defaults for variance, density, motion, type, and imagery.

Earned: every exception, expressive gesture, promotion verdict, and claim of model effectiveness.

02 Four starting points

Choose by job, not by aesthetic.

Modes are categorical contracts. They are not themes, rankings, or a maturity ladder.

03 The working loop

From a real brief to a reviewable receipt.

DES is practice, references, and executable proof—not a theme generator.

  1. 1
    Read the product

    Find the real audience, task, state, authority, and consequence.

  2. 2
    Select a mode

    Name the task anatomy before choosing a visual direction.

  3. 3
    Declare the tuple

    Record harness, requested and served model, tier, and capabilities.

  4. 4
    Build a real state

    Use production-shaped content and functional interactions.

  5. 5
    Capture proof

    Audit multiple widths, keyboard flow, contrast, overflow, and errors.

  6. 6
    Promote deliberately

    Independent seeing or explicit human acceptance closes the loop.
